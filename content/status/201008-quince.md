Johann Kuntz recommended [Chaenomeles cathayensis](https://pfaf.org/USER/Plant.aspx?LatinName=Chaenomeles+cathayensis) on [Edimentals Facebook group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/273637002647408/) as best fruiting ornamental quince. Also, advised using crushed eggshells to reduce acidity, rather than a metric tonne of sugar.
