A big thank you to Steve Wilson and The People’s Orchard @peoplesorchardstdogs for the Grafting Workshop today. Such a sense of achievement from grafting two apple trees. The scions were the Bubblegum Tree from Poppit Sands (can’t remember its official name!) and Ashmead’s Kernel, both on an MM106 rootstock. I potted them as soon as I got home, you can just about make them out in the photo in the polytunnel.
